About Dataset

In this project, we will work with the advertising data of a marketing agency to identify potential opportunities for growth and recommendations for the advertisement strategy.

The data consists of 10 variables: 'Daily Time Spent on Site', 'Age', 'Area Income', 'Daily Internet Usage', 'Ad Topic Line', 'City', 'Male', 'Country', Timestamp' and 'Clicked on Ad'. The main variable we are interested in is 'Clicked on Ad'. This variable can have two possible outcomes: 0 and 1 where 0 refers to the case where a user didn't click the advertisement, while 1 refers to the scenario where a user clicks the advertisement.

Dataset: Kaggle

Business Task

Identify potential opportunities for growth and recommendations for the advertisement strategy improvement based on trends in data.

Data Cleaning and Transformation

Performing Basic Exploratory Analysis in SQL

Checking Average Age and Click Rate

Select AVG(age) as Average_age, AVG(clicked) as Click_rate 
FROM [Portfolio Project]..advertising_ef

Checking distribution by Gender

SELECT COUNT(gender)
FROM [Portfolio Project]..advertising_ef
GROUP BY gender

Checking distribution by age

SELECT Age_group,AVG(clicked) 
FROM [Portfolio Project]..advertising_ef$
group by Age_group
ORDER BY 1

Checking trends in Countries

SELECT Country,AVG(clicked)
FROM [Portfolio Project]..advertising_ef
group by country
ORDER BY 2 desc

VISUALIZATION

FINDINGS/SUGGESTIONS

  • There is a clear co-relation between the age of the user and the likelihood of him clicking on the ad.
    Target people over the age of 50 and avoid people below the age of 30.
  • People spending less than 60 minutes on the site are far more likely to click on the ad
  • There is no notable change in the effectiveness of the ads throughout the day.
  • Users from a Rich neighbourhood are far less likely to Click on the ad.
  • Users who spent more than 3 hours on the internet are far less likely to click on the ad
